Analysis

1. EE is the largest mobile network in the UK with 32 million subscribers, making up about 32% of the market.

2. O2 and Vodafone are also major players with 24 and 18 million subscribers, respectively.

3. Many users have multiple SIM cards, which is why total subscribers exceed the UK population (~68 million).

4. Smaller networks such as Sky Mobile, Tesco Mobile, and other MVNOs serve niche markets, mainly prepaid or value-focused customers.

5. The market is competitive, but postpaid & prepaid combined networks dominate overall subscriber numbers.
