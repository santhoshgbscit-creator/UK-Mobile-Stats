Rough Analysis

EE is the largest network in the UK, followed by O2 and Vodafone.

Many users have multiple SIMs, so the total number of subscribers exceeds the UK population.

Smaller networks like Sky Mobile and Tesco Mobile serve niche markets or prepaid users.
